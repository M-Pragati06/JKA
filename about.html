Japan Karate Association Satara

JKA Satara is an official branch of the Japan Karate Association, the world's most prestigious karate organization. Founded in 2001, we have been dedicated to preserving and teaching authentic Shotokan Karate as developed by Master Gichin Funakoshi, the father of modern karate.

Our dojo follows the traditional teachings while incorporating modern training methodologies to help students of all ages and abilities achieve their full potential. Over the years, we've trained thousands of students, producing national and international champions while maintaining our core philosophy of character development through martial arts.

Certified Instructors

All our instructors are JKA certified with decades of combined experience.

Proven Results

Over 50 national champions trained at our dojo in the past decade.

Our Mission

To provide authentic Shotokan Karate training that develops not just physical skills but also cultivates discipline, respect, and personal growth in every student. We aim to preserve the traditional values of karate while making it accessible to the modern practitioner.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence for martial arts training in Maharashtra, producing well-rounded individuals who embody the spirit of karate in their daily lives. We strive to expand our reach while maintaining the highest standards of instruction and integrity.

Our History

Journey of JKA Satara through the years

2001

JKA Satara was established as the first official Japan Karate Association branch in the region, founded by Sensei Virendra Pardeshi.

2004

Our first student won a gold medal at the National Karate Championship, putting JKA Satara on the national map.

2008

Moved to our current 5000 sq.ft. facility with state-of-the-art training equipment and dedicated spaces for different age groups.

2010

Celebrated our 10th anniversary with a special seminar featuring JKA Japan Chief Instructor Anand Ratna.

2016

Launched our youth development program which has since trained over 500 children in karate and life skills.

2024

Recognized as "Best Karate Dojo in Maharashtra" by the Maharashtra Martial Arts Association.

Our Training Philosophy

The JKA Satara approach to martial arts education

Character Development

We believe karate is a way of life that builds discipline, respect, and perseverance that extends beyond the dojo.

Technical Excellence

Our training emphasizes perfecting the fundamental techniques of Shotokan Karate as taught by JKA Japan.

Holistic Approach

We develop the whole person - physical fitness, mental focus, and spiritual growth through traditional training.
